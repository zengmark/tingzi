01 — 关于About

关于我About Me

从场景、行为和反馈出发，梳理更清晰的用户路径。Starting from scenarios, behaviors and feedback to map clearer user journeys.

我相信好的设计是无形的——它不喧哗，却让每一次交互都自然而然。我把字体、色彩、组件和图形语言整理成可延展的系统，让产品在不同媒介间保持一致的叙事。 I believe great design is invisible — quiet, yet making every interaction feel natural. I organize type, color, components and graphics into extensible systems that keep a consistent narrative across media.
